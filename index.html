Born Physical/Studied Digitally: A consortium funded by the National Science Foundation

Learn More

Unlock Hidden Data

We digitize and structure data trapped in physical archives—like seismic traces, court records, and historical documents—so they can power new scientific discovery and public insight.

Build Open Tools

Our platform integrates cutting-edge technologies for document processing, metadata extraction, and knowledge graph creation—all open-source and built for academic and public use.

Grow the Community

Through partnerships, courses, and training, we empower students, archivists, and researchers—especially from underserved communities—to participate in the future of digital scholarship.
